It's not bad, and it feels kinda bad to say I *disliked* it considering it's the first bullet hell, but to be honest there's not much. The controls are quite clunky, I find the visuals to be less charming than the first game, the story is, there, I guess. I don't know, most definitely feels like the weakest of all the games.
