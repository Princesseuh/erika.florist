I do not understand what people see in this game, it's so unfun. There's a million cutscenes with frankly not very inspiring dialogue for the most part. You spend most of the game waiting. The only redeeming factor is that when you get to play, once in a while, it doesn't feel too bad and some parts of the game are ok. Otherwise, I'd rather be doing anything else.
