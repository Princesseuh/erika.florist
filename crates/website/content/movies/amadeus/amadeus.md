Really good! I find it incredibly funny to read critics of this online because it seems like half of them have neither understood or seen the movie.

The acting in this from both Tom Hulce and F. Murray Abraham in this is incredible, and really sells the relationship between the characters.

I watched the Director's Cut, but looking at differences online, the changes don't bother me. The famous scene with Salieri and Constanze is a tough one, it does helps the characterization a lot and the movie clearly expects it to play out (see: the end of the movie), but it also makes Salieri look more evil than needed, especially through a modern lens.

Anyway, loved it
