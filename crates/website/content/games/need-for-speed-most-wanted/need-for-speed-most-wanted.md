It holds up surprisingly well, and with a mod or two also honesty looks amazing even today.

However, the pacing of the game is super slow, and on one hand it's great how it starts easy and gets harder, etc, but when you're "good" at the game, you honestly just feel very bored for a long time, both in races and outside of them. For instance, you spend so much time at low heat where the cops are just so dumb and slow.

It's a loved, but it could've been a very high liked just as easily.
