It's still really good, but I STRONGLY STRONGLY recommend a mod that either rebalance the progression or shorten races / reduce the number of laps. The vanilla game is so insanely padded in length and repetitive races.

In some ways, it didn't age as well as Most Wanted did, but everything that was good about it, well, is still as good as it was back then! Customizing your car and racing through the streets of the city is still just so cool.
