Really liked the beginning, but I found that towards the end the story got a bit too much in the way of my enjoyment, I felt like it would've been possible to tell an actual story with this format that would've been awesome, but it's not really that.

I don't mind the 2019 Netflix-show vibes, mind you, but it just didn't feel compelling enough. The minigames are quite fun for the most part and I overall still enjoyed my time!
