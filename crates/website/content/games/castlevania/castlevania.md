I think the people who hate this are honestly tripping. Like, okay, yes, the combat and the camera are clunky af, but there's such charm to everything else! The *ambiance*, the cutscenes, the visuals, the soundtrack etc.

The latter part of the game is quite weaker than the beginning, but still, incredible and very fun.
