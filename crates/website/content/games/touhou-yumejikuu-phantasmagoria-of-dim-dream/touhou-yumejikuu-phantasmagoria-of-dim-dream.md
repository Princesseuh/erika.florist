Different style again for this one! It might be fun if you take it *very* seriously, you play with someone else etc. but in single player, it's honestly quite boring. The patterns are really easy and simple for the most part, or you can't avoid them because of the other player, which tend to feel cheap. The background are non-existent, and strangely enough, I found the music in this one less interesting, but still alright.
