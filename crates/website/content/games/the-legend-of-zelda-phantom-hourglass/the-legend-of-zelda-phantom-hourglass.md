It's pretty good overall, but the flaws kinda become very tiresome quickly. I really don't like the temple of the ocean king, the sea travel is almost always unnecessarily long (and the fast travel system is a bit clunky for modern times, though it's in-universe neat), the controls are not always very responsive, etc.

I think when I was a child the stamping made me scream and not sure I've reached such a peak since, iykyk (I did, playing 999 not too long after)
